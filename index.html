How to play

the goal is the first player can achive 100 total score wins. Rules are simple. Player 1 start rolling the dice. The point on the dice will add to current. you have to option : 1. HOLD. when the player hold, the current score will add to total score and its's next player turn. 2. Roll dice again. You can continiue rolling but if dice show 1 you will lose your score and it's next player turn.
